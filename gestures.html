grstures

OK

How to do it :
Join your thumb and index finger to form a loop and then extend the remaining three fingers up.

OK (Surface)

How to do it :
Communicate “OK” above water touching the top of your head with your fingertips.

Down / Descend

How to do it :
Do the universal thumbs-down hand signal.

Problem

How to do it :
With an open, flat hand facing downwards, rotate it slowly side by side as if to say “so-so.”

Stop / Hold

How to do it :
Hold a flat hand with the palm facing forward or hold your forearm up and make a fist with the palm side facing forward.

Watch / Look

How to do it :
Hold up the “peace” sign and point to your eyes.

Follow Me

How to do it :
If I will lead the way, first point to myself, the other hand to follow, on behalf of myself to lead the way, if the other way is the latent partner to lead the way.

Low on Air

How to do it :
Hold a closed fist against your chest.

Out of Air

How to do it :
Move a flat hand across your throat in a slicing motion.
